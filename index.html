FAQ

How long does it take to develop a project?

+

The development time depends on the complexity and scope of the project. A simple landing page can be created in 1-2 weeks, while a comprehensive web application may take from 3 to 6 months. After analyzing the requirements, we provide accurate deadlines for implementation.

What technology stack are you using?

+

We use modern technologies, including JavaScript (React, Vue.js, Node.js), Python (Django, Flask), PHP (Laravel), databases (MySQL, PostgreSQL, MongoDB), as well as DevOps tools (Docker, AWS, Git). The technology stack is selected based on the requirements of the project.

Do you provide technical support after the launch?

+

Yes, we offer various technical support packages after the project is launched. This may include bug fixes, security updates, consultations, and the addition of new functionality. The terms of support are discussed individually for each project.

How does the payment process work?

+

We work on a flexible payment model. For small projects, an advance payment of 50% and 50% is possible after the completion of the project. For large projects, we break down the payment by stages: analysis, design, development, testing. All conditions are fixed in the contract.
